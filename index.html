This page is the start page of my thesis project. In this blog, I will kick off my project, plan the thesis structure and works, track the progress, and deliver thesis promptly.

My Goal

Main idea of my graduation thesis is that: Location-based service based on massive trajectories and geo-spatial semantic knowledge. I will focus on the following topics:

Large-scale Trajectory Analysis
Knowledge Base and Knowledge Data Management
Multi-source Data Fusion
Location Recommendation
